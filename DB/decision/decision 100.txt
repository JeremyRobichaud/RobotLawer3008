Conclusion

 

[135]      On the first count in the indictment, first degree murder, I find S.R.B. not guilty of the offence as charged but guilty of the included offence of manslaughter. On the second count, aggravated sexual assault, I find her guilty. On the third count, kidnapping, I find her guilty.
