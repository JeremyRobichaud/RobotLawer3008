Order to Stand Trial

[104]      Pursuant to s. 548(1)(a) of the Criminal Code, there is sufficient evidence to put the three accused:  Trong Minh Nguyen, Henry Le Nguyen, and Byran James Balla on trial for the offence charged – conspiracy to commit the murder of  Vinh Truong.  There is also sufficient evidence to put the accused Trong Minh Nguyen on trial for counselling to commit murder.  I order all three accused to stand trial for the offences for which they are charged.

 

Heard on the 18th, 19th, 20th, 21st, 22nd days of November, 2013, and the 20th day of December 2013.

Dated at the City of Calgary, Alberta this 24th day of February, 2014.
